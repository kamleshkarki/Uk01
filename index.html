History of Uttarakhand

9 November 2000

Uttarakhand became the 27th state of the Indian Republic, Uttarakhand was earlier called Uttaranchal. The divine site of God still exists in Uttarakhand, so it is also known as the land of the gods, the state is divided into 13 districts, including Kumaon and Garhwal. The winter capital of Uttarakhand is Dehradun, which is the largest city in the state. On 4 March 2020, Gairsain was declared the summer capital of the state. Which comes in Chamoli district.

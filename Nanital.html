Nanital

Nainital is a popular hill station in the state of Uttarakhand. The city is situated in a valley, a wonderful view of nature which adds to this scene of nature. it is a mountainous area, surrounded by lakes and Talos. 

In January and February, Nainital remains covered with snow. The Nainital northern chronology is between 28 -51 degrees and 26 -37 degrees, it's area is 1709063 acres. To the north of this is Almora and Garhwal district, between Almora and Nainital, Kumania, Koshi rivers flow.

Here many types of water wind are found in the mountains, it is always very cold in the snowy places, and due to the high peaks are also cool in summer, especially in the morning and evening, there is great enjoyment in the mountains.

In Nainital, the mercury of temperature does not exceed 30 degrees in summer and goes up to 14 to 2 degrees in winter. In summer, Nainital, Chaubatia, Binsar, etc. mountains, Almora in the rainy season, and Haldwani in Jaro seem very attractive, which captivates the minds of the visitors. The market of Nainital is very small and attractive, which is very much liked by the visitors.

Nainital is quite famous for places of interest and fast food. And the best time to travel is from March to April.

  • Tiffin Top
  • China Peak
  • Camels Back
